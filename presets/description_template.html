A Little bit about me...

Welcome to my autograph page. I am Kim and I am from Manchester, England. I started collecting Autographs around about Summer 1999. I have been a keen collector ever since my friend got me Paul Daniels and Debbie McGee's autographs in Blackpool in early 1999. Since then I have been addicted! I am primarily a collector but a couple of years back I started swapping and selling a few duplicates I had, I had such great feedback that I carried on selling and supplying many other collectors. I opened this account to share my collection with the World and now I sell autographs full time! I am so lucky I can do my passion as a job! I hope you can find the time to have a look at my complete collection, I am sure if you are a collector, like me, you will find something of interest!
